VISION

Virtual Integrated Supply-chain Improvement with Optimized Networking

A groundbreaking R&D project to build the next generation of intelligent supply chains for the construction, mining, and aerospace industries.

The Challenge

Modern supply chains are fragmented. This leads to critical inefficiencies in material tracking, costly delivery delays, and poor resource utilization. Compounding this, industries face mounting pressure to improve sustainability and guarantee data security.

  • Inefficiency: Disparate systems prevent real-time decision-making and optimal resource use.
  • Sustainability: A growing imperative to reduce carbon footprints and minimize material waste.
  • Security: A critical need for data integrity, provenance, and traceability across all partners.

Our Solution

VISION’s primary goal is to create a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) that provides a comprehensive, real-time, and predictive view of the entire supply chain. By integrating advanced technologies, we enable smarter, faster, and more secure operations.

Core Technologies

Digital Twins (DT)

Creating virtual replicas of the physical supply chain for real-time monitoring, simulation, and proactive problem-solving.

Augmented Reality (AR)

Deploying AR for enhanced visualization, efficient routing, and load optimization to reduce errors and boost productivity.

Ultra-Wideband (UWB)

Implementing UWB for precise, sub-10cm real-time 3D location tracking of materials, assets, and personnel.

Market Impact & Use Cases

Aerospace

Enhancing component traceability to guarantee quality, ensure compliance, and mitigate risks from counterfeit parts.

Construction

Streamlining material handling to reduce waste (saving millions annually) and improve resource utilization.

Mining

Using digital twins for precise mine site modeling and route optimization to enhance efficiency and reduce risk.

Warehouse & Logistics

Optimizing cargo loading and ensuring inventory accuracy with a combination of UWB, digital twins, and AR.
